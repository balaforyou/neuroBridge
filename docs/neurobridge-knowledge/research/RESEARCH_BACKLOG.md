## NB-024: Knowledge Index Framework

* Story ID: NB-024
* Title: Knowledge Index Framework
* Epic: Knowledge Management & Research Enablement
* Priority: Medium
* Status: Backlog

### Objective

Create a structured cross-reference layer between observations, scaffolds, skills, domains, ideas, backlog items, activities, and future research artifacts.

### Problem

Knowledge is currently captured in separate repositories:

* OBSERVATIONS.md
* SCAFFOLDS.md
* IDEAS.md
* BACKLOG.md
* ARCHITECTURE_NOTES.md

As NeuroBridge grows, relationships between these artifacts become difficult to discover manually.

### Proposed Solution

Introduce a Knowledge Index that links:

Observation
↓
Scaffold
↓
Skill
↓
Domain
↓
Activity
↓
Backlog Item
↓
Research Insight

### Example

Observation:

* O-001 Left-Right Confusion

Linked Scaffold:

* S-001 Color Cue Scaffold

Linked Skill:

* left-right

Linked Cognitive Target:

* spatial-orientation

Linked Domain:

* attention

Linked Activity:

* Directions Activity

Linked Backlog:

* NB-130 Adaptive Game Selection

### Future Capabilities

* Search observations by skill
* Search scaffolds by domain
* Identify successful scaffold patterns
* Trace rationale behind product decisions
* Support research paper preparation
* Support future AI-assisted recommendations
* Support therapist and teacher collaboration

### Deliverables

Phase 1

* Manual markdown-based knowledge index

Phase 2

* Structured JSON/index file

Phase 3

* Searchable UI

Phase 4

* AI-assisted knowledge exploration

### Notes

This framework serves as the bridge between:

* Learning Science
* Product Design
* Parent Observations
* Analytics
* Research Publications

and preserves institutional knowledge as NeuroBridge evolves.
